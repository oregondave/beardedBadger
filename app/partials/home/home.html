Because unicorns are soft. And a little bit weird.

When I hear the term unicorn, I envision some magical creature with wondrous powers bestowed upon it for no reason other than the pure dumb luck of being born a unicorn. Over the years I have met a handful of designers & developers that would meet that criteria. I am not one of them. Becoming a competent, professional designer took years of hard work & training. Same goes for HTML, Javascript/jQuery, Responsive Design & UX. Maybe it came a little easier to me than it would for some random guy or gal plucked out of a crowd - I'm not sure. But becoming 'good' at what I do took hard work, determination & more than a little grit and stubbornness. I sir, am no unicorn. I'd rather be a badger any day.

Hi. I'm Dave.

I make things for the web. It amazes me. The combination of html, css and javascript is like some kind of sorcery powered by caffiene, beards and elf magic. We cosume it with these magical little devices we all carry around in our pockets, purses and bags. How could I not want to be a part of that?

Over the years I've been a graphic designer, web desgner, GUI designer, Flash designer, UI designer, frontend designer, Design Director, Creative Director and UX designer as well as a handful of things you probably wouldn't believe if I told you. Put all that in a pot, stir it around, and that's what I do. For lack of a better term, I'll just say web designer. I'm Dave, and I'm a web designer.
